  • METHOD V (Unearthed Arcana Human Only:
    Rolling social class may limit your class choices unless you chose method V at the beginning.
    Method V players can be of any social class (Cavaliers and Paladins (UA version) are 0L if lower than UMC.
    All other classes will be a random social class, though their class choice will be valid regardless.
    This can be considered your actual social class, with your character as an exception, or your current class is the
    minimum, and what is rolled here is ignored, or could be considered where the character 'started' their career.


  • IGNORING SOCIAL CLASS:

    • Ignoring social class forces you to MUC: Middle Upper Class, allowing any class choice your abilities/race allow.
    Your DM may choose to ignore Social Class entirely, this will enable the minimum for all options.
